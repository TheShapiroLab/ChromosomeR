fRS1346 and fRS1033 were omitted in this IC50 analysis as their IC50 was not able to be accurately identified due to low growth in all but the lowest concentration of FLZ (not counting the no-drug well), reflecting a slight decrease in MIC from the other strains, as the other strains grew modestly in the second-to-lowest FLZ concentration.
